About Satta Matka

Satta or Matka betting is a type of lottery which initially included wagering on the opening and shutting paces of cotton transmitted from the New York Cotton Trade. It begins before the time of Indian autonomy when it was known as Ankada Jugar. During the 1960s, the framework was supplanted with different methods for creating arbitrary numbers, including pulling slips from an enormous pottery pot known as a matka, or managing playing a game of cards.
